# Idealism

## Hanzi

善為士者
不武。

善戰者
不怒。

善勝敵者
弗與。

善用人者
為之下。

是謂不諍之德，
是謂用人，
是謂配天，
古之極也。

## English

To be civilized,
don't be warlike.

To be a good warrior,
don’t get angry.

To be good at defeating the enemy,
don't engage them.

To be a good commander,
demote yourself.

It's about nonviolence,
it's about cooperation,
it's about partnering with the universe,
the ancient ideal.
