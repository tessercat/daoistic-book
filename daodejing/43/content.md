# Progress

## Hanzi

天下之至柔
馳騁於天下之至堅。

無有
入於無閒。

吾是以知
無為之有益也。

不言之教，
無為之益，
天下希能及之矣。

## English

The meekest
overtake the mightiest.

The insubstantial
permeates the impenetrable.

So I ponder
the substantial benefit of doing nothing.

I learn from silence,
get ahead by doing nothing,
and the rest of the world can barely keep up.
